### Ingredients

* 2 cans of chickpeas (2x400g)
* 30g sezame seeds
* 70-100g cold water
* 2-3 Tbsp aceto balsamico, lime or lemon juice
* 3 Tbsp olive oil
* 1 tsp salt

### Method

1. Roast the sezame seeds in a pan for 3-5 min and grind them.
2. Mix all the ingredients in the food processor.
